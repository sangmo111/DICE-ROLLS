Welcome to the Dice Roller

This interactive page allows you to roll dice and obtain statistical results based on your selections. Here's a brief overview of its functionality:

  • Select the number of dice you want to roll (One, Two, or Three).
  • Enter the number of times you want to roll the dice.
  • Click on the dice image to generate results.
  • The page will then display various statistics including mean, median, mode, and frequency of doubles and triples. Additionally, you'll see the cumulative results of the dice rolls and their respective frequencies in tables.
